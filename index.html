About Us

Janajagriti Secondary School Chitwan was established in 1962 AD (2019 BS) as a community based educational institution, situated in Pithuwa, Chitwan District of Nepal. It is affiliated to the National Examination Board (NEB) and approved by the Ministry of Education. It offers programs from ECD to Grade 10 and Plus Two in Pure Management, Management with Computer, and Education streams.

  • Cafeteria, Library, Sports, Science & Computer Labs
  • Multimedia, Counseling & Scholarships
  • Dance, Music, Educational Tours, and more
